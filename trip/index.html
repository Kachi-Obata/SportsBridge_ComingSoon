Key Notes — You could read these before you travel
NYC — One store, three sports
NYC Racquet Sports at Herald Square (157A W 35th St) is your single most efficient stop for Lawn Tennis, Badminton AND Squash in New York.

It's rated the #1 racquet sports store in NYC on both Yelp and Google, not to mention it's conveniently located on the same street as New York Golf Center.

You could cover all three racket sports.
NYC — Wilson Flagship
Wilson's NYC Flagship at 594 Broadway (SoHo) is apparently the only Wilson brand store in New York.

From what I can gather it's a 3 floor experience with an indoor play area and exclusive products you "cannot buy anywhere else".

I reckon it's a must-visit for Lawn Tennis brand engagement.
Atlanta — PGA TOUR Superstore is at home
Atlanta is PGA TOUR Superstore's home city. Their headquarters are in Roswell, metro Atlanta.

Also, the brand new Buckhead flagship (3255 Peachtree Rd NE) opened March 7, 2026, just weeks before your visit. It's 30,000 square ft with TrackMan tech, 5 fitting bays, a putting green, and 65,000+ club and shaft combinations.

This is literally one of the most advanced golf retail experiences in the US and is a significant stop. You could have some fun here.
Atlanta — Your Serve Tennis has 10 locations
From what I could gather, Your Serve Tennis has 10 locations across metro Atlanta.

The Buckhead location (3749 Roswell Rd) is listed as the priority because it's closest to the PGA TOUR Superstore Buckhead — you can do both in the same trip.

You can find the additional locations in the Atlanta view for reference though.
London — Wigmore Sports covers three sports in one visit
Wigmore Sports at 39 Wigmore Street, Marylebone might be the single most important racket sports stop in London.

It's an official Yonex stockist and carries over 2,000 rackets across Lawn Tennis, Badminton and Squash.

It's near Bond Street tube station and covers all three racket sports in one visit.

Open Mon–Sat 10am–6pm, Thursday until 7pm.
London — Tennisnuts is in Zone 6
I listed Tennisnuts (Northwood Hills, Middlesex) as a non-priority alternative for Tennis, Badminton and Squash.

It has a hitting room to demo rackets before buying, which is a unique feature. However, it's in Zone 6 on the Metropolitan Line — significantly further from central London than Wigmore Sports.

Since Wigmore Sports covers the same brands more centrally, Tennisnuts is only worth visiting if you have a free day with nothing better to do.
Yonex UK — I couldn't find a physical store, brand meeting requires prior arrangement
Yonex has a UK subsidiary (Yonex UK Ltd) but no brand-owned retail store that can be walked into.

If you want to arrange a trade or partnership meeting with Yonex UK, this would need to be set up in advance via email or phone before you get to London.

Wigmore Sports is an official Yonex stockist and may be the best physical point of contact for Yonex products in London.
Brand appointments (Callaway, TaylorMade, PING, Titleist)
As in New York, but these golf brands don't seem to have standalone flagship stores in Atlanta or London that can simply be walked into.

Any brand-level meetings or appointments need to be arranged in advance directly with the brand's regional representatives.

The retailers listed in each city (PGA TOUR Superstore, Capital Golf, American Golf) all carry the full ranges of these brands and are your best bet at a physical access point.
